How It Works

  1. Enter your API keys (Airtable, SerpAPI, Apollo.io)
  2. Click "Connect to Airtable" to load your candidates and table structure
  3. Choose enrichment mode:
    • • Single: Test on one candidate
    • • Missing Data: Only enrich profiles with missing information
    • • All: Process every candidate (use with caution!)
  4. The tool will automatically search for and update:
    • 📚 Google Scholar Profile
    • 🎓 PhD Affiliation (university - creates/links in Affiliations table)
    • 🏢 Current Company (creates/links in Companies table)
    • 📧 Email Address (via Apollo.io)
    • 💼 LinkedIn Profile
  5. All changes are automatically saved to your Airtable with proper linking

💡 Tip: Start with a single candidate to verify everything works correctly before running batch operations!
